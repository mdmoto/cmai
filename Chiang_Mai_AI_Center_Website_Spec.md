# Chiang Mai AI Center Website Specification

## Positioning

Chiang Mai AI Center is **not** a coworking space. It is a business
infrastructure platform for international companies entering Thailand.

Design style: - Apple / Stripe / Linear - Minimal, premium, spacious -
White background, black typography - Technology-focused - Responsive
(desktop/tablet/mobile) - English first, multilingual-ready
